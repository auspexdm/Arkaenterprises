Government subsidy

The government pays a big part of your system.

Under PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana, residential rooftop solar gets direct Central Financial Assistance. We handle the application, documentation and net-metering approval for you.

Residential rooftop

Central Financial Assistance (CFA) — per household
1 kW system₹30,000
2 kW system₹60,000
3 kW & above₹78,000
Documents: electricity bill · Aadhaar · PAN · bank details · roof ownership proof. Subject to scheme terms & DISCOM net-metering approval.

Housing societies (RWA / CHS)

Rooftop solar for common facilities — lifts, water pumps, common lighting, clubhouse and CCTV — cuts common-area bills by 50–90%. Societies get ₹18,000 per kW subsidy under PM Surya Ghar.
